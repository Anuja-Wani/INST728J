Sanskar Bharti Rangoli


Rangoli is the traditional Indian art form which can be drawn in different ways. The broad categories include sanskar bharti rangoli and dotted rangoli. These designs have been traditionally drawn outside each home everyday, specially in western Indian regions like Maharashtra and Gujarath. The rangoli is considered to be auspicious and has more importance during festivals.
